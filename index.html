The ProGuard 6kg CO₂ replaces the CF-CO2-5KG in our commercial range, adding 20% more discharge time while reducing overall weight by 400g. It's the unit our commercial customers have been asking for — powerful enough for server rooms and commercial kitchen hood suppression, light enough for any member of staff to handle confidently.

Target customer profile: commercial kitchen operators, facilities managers in multi-floor office and retail, and IT infrastructure teams. Positioning: "the professional's choice — more power, less weight, fully compliant."

The key commercial differentiator over the current market leader (Kidde 6kg CO₂, RRP £104) is price: at £89.50 RRP we're £14.50 under and 14% lighter. The updated discharge horn also passes the revised EN3 noise tests without modification — no additional PPE signage required, unlike several competitors.
